Whitepaper

What is Wayfinder?

It's More than Protocol, It's a Platform.

A Defi Farmer's Market

Farming as a service has proven to be a very effective way to invest in Defi. Our Platform provides Farmer and Money Managers with everything they would need to allow their followers to invest in their talents and automate the rest.

Sustainability

We're building our protocol with sustainability in mind. Our first major investments planned are to purchase validator nodes to provide sustainable income to our users.

Leveraged Farming

Waypoints automate the process of finding and moving your stables to find the best passive yield.

Investments into New Technology

New opportunities for investments will be proposed and voted on by those who stake in the treasury. Proposals that get the majority of votes receives the funding.

Features

Introducing Waypoints

Waypoints are programmable vaults that allow the users to easily take advantage of advanced strategies that would otherwise difficult or impossible for the newbie. There are 3 different types of Waypoints.

Choose your Journey!

Waypoints allow you to easily choose how to put your money to work. You can choose from safer stablecoin options or our actively managed Waypoints.

Simplified Investing, Advanced Results

Trying to navigate your way around Defi can be a very risky and costly learning experience. Let our W aypoints take care of that for you.

Claim Airdrops and Receive Rewards

Wen Airdrop?? All the time!. Investment managers of actively managed Waypoints may choose to reward and incentivize stakers via airdrops.

How it works

Our System

1

Buy and Stake

Swap for our token at your preferred DEX.

2

Treasury AutoSwap

Once you've staked, the treasury automatically swaps the cash value of your deposit to the target strategy tokens.

3

Both Automated and Actively Managed Waypoints employ the strategies you've chosen.

4

Claim Rewards Receive Airdrops
