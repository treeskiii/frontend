What is Wayfinder?

Wayfinder is project dedicated to creating a friendly ecosystem for cryptocurrency investment and unlocking the cross-project potential that exists by developing technologies that enable building both simple and complex investment strategies usable by newbies and seasoned pros alike in a consistent standardized way.

Why is Wayfinder Special?

Wayfinder is special because we are bringing real innovation to the decentralized finance space, rather than just another protocol borrowing someone else's code. We will provide the foundational technology, invest our capital into supporting that technology, and then enable other projects to use it. There are countless opportunities in the cryptocurrency space, Wayfinder's goal is to unify those opportunities in a way that makes them accessible and interoperable to everyone.

Introducing Waypoints

A Waypoint is an on-chain API platform that enables crowd sourcing and creation of both automated and custodial investment strategies. Users of Wayfinder Finance can then mix, match, and combine these strategies into a portfolio that matches their needs.

There are 2 Main Types of Waypoints.

Type 1:

Simple

Pragmatic strategies premade by Wayfinder.

Automated

The strategy is automated by the logic of the Waypoint.

Bluechips

Choose from a selection of Blue Chip tokens such as Ethereum, Bitcoin, and Solana.

Leveraged Stable Coins

Stake your tokens, choose what percentage of your portfolio you want to leverage, and let Waypoint do the rest!

Type 2:

Actively Managed

Funds Traded by Waypoint Manager.

Custom Strategies

Let the manager use their knowledge and talents to increase your ROI.

Approved Tokens Only

Managers will only be able to interact with approved contracts by proxy through the Waypoint which controls the assets. Users know upfront what managers can or can't do.

Protect Against Rugs

Managers never have direct access to funds. They can only control the interactions with pre-approved token contracts. Fees and profits are automatically calculated and distributed for everyone.

Truly Distributed

Decentralized DeFi isn't about just "another fork" of someone's else's work. It is building things designed to be built off of to create even more opportunities for everyone.

Airdrops

When a Waypoint profits, users claim their reward based on their share of the total pool when combined with other investors, minus fees. Waypoint owners can even reallocate their fees as airdrops to their users.

NFT Wallet Portfolio's

Wayfinder tracks a user's investments via a "portfolio" NFT This allows your investment to also be an asset to use as collateral for a loan.

Choose Your Journey

Waypoints allow you to easily choose how to put your money to work. You can choose from safer stablecoin options or our actively managed Waypoints.
